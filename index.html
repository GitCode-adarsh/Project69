Welcome To Salwan Public School

A Warm Welcome to the Website of Salwan Public School Mayur Vihar. The vision of our school is based on innovative learning methodology, continuous improvement, cultivation of practical skills and an unwavering commitment to academic quality.The Salwan schools are committed to the intellectual growth of humanity and occupies a place of eminence in the country by bringing about awareness in the mind of child learning through hand on experience.

Campus Life

Arts

Come and See our School,Arts

To discover the undiscovered talents and innovative students, Art exhibition and competitions are regularly organized. It is about encouraging, nurturing and showcasing every child's natural creative flair. Through these contests students learn to explore the extent of their imagination and express themselves without any fear. Some of young artist’s of our school, who are too young to even understand the meaning of life, have achieved the greater levels of creativity and refinement

Sports

Come and See our School,Sports

To encourage the participation of more and more students in sports and physical activities, CBSE has been conducting sports events for the schools which are organized at Cluster (Zonal) and National level every year under various age groups. This year it was organized at Chhatrasaal stadium from 14th to 15th Oct 2019. Students from Salwan Public School, Mayur Vihar, showcased their agility and strength while participating in the event and astounded everyone through their commendable performances and by winning various medals in different categories.

SILICO BATTLES
Techincal Skill

To hone the technical skills a few students participated in 'Silico Battles', organised by Ahlcon Public School on 31st Oct and 1st Nov 2019. Two students Vaishnavi Sinha and Aniket Singh of class XI A won the first prize in E-Waste Management, where they made working models of 'Garbage Collector/Smart Bin' , 'Mopper' and "Vaccum Cleaner", with the e-waste available in school. Kshitiz Prakash of Class XI A won 3rd prize in Dexterity, in which he created an app using App inventor and presented the same to the Judges and the audience. And last but not the least, Prashant NIgam of class XI A, also won 3rd Prize in 'RESPAWN PC".
